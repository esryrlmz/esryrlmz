Privacy Policy for Islamic Counter

Effective Date: January 16, 2025
Last Updated: January 16, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Islamic Counter ("we," "our," or "us") handles information in our mobile application Islamic Counter - Digital Tasbeeh (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Islamic Counter is designed with privacy in mind. We want to be clear about what we do NOT do:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.
  • No User Accounts: Our app does not require user registration, login, or account creation.
  • No Online Data Storage: We do not store any of your data on our servers or in the cloud.
  • No Location Tracking: We do not access or collect your location information.
  • No Device Information: We do not collect information about your device, IP address, or usage analytics.
  • No Third-Party Data Sharing: Since we don't collect data, we have nothing to share with third parties.

Local Data Storage

Islamic Counter stores your prayer counters and custom prayers locally on your device only:

  • Prayer Data: Your prayer names, counter values, and target goals are stored locally in your device's database.
  • Settings: Your app preferences and reminder settings are stored locally on your device.
  • No Backup: This local data is not backed up to any external servers or cloud services.
  • Device Only: All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or anyone else.

Permissions

Our app requests minimal permissions:

  • Vibration: To provide haptic feedback when you tap the counter (optional).
  • Notifications: To send you prayer reminders if you enable them (optional).
  • Storage: To save your prayer data locally on your device.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit your personal information.

Data Security

Since all your data remains on your device:

  • Your data is as secure as your device's security measures.
  • We recommend using your device's built-in security features (screen lock, encryption, etc.).
  • If you uninstall the app, all your local data will be permanently deleted.

Children's Privacy

Islamic Counter does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Advertising

Currently, Islamic Counter may display advertisements through Google AdMob. Please note:

  • We do not collect or share your personal data with advertisers.
  • Ad networks may use their own technologies to serve relevant ads.
  • You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Ads are served based on app content, not personal data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
  • Notify users through the app if there are significant changes.
  • Continue to maintain our commitment to not collecting personal data.

Data Deletion

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing for us to delete. To remove your local data:

  • Simply uninstall the Islamic Counter app from your device.
  • All your prayer counters and settings will be permanently removed.

International Users

Islamic Counter is available worldwide. Since we don't collect or process personal data, international data transfer laws do not apply to our app.

Since we don't collect personal information, traditional data rights (access, correction, deletion, etc.) don't apply. However:

  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app.
  • You can disable notifications and vibrations in app settings.

Compliance

  • Google Play Store requirements
  • General privacy best practices
  • Our commitment to user privacy

Summary

In simple terms:

  • We don't collect your personal information
  • Everything stays on your phone
  • No accounts or registration required
  • No data is sent to our servers
